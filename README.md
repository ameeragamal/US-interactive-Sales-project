# 👟 Adidas US Interactive Sales Dashboard

An interactive data visualization platform providing deep-dive analytics into Adidas US sales performance. This dashboard transforms transactional data into strategic insights regarding regional profitability, product categories, and retail channel efficiency.

---

## 📊 Business Insights Overview

This dashboard tracks over **2.4M units sold** and **$332M in total profit**, offering a multi-dimensional view of the business:

### 1. Product Performance (Profit per Product)
* **Leader:** *Men's Street Footwear* is the top profit driver ($82.8M).
* **Categories:** Breaks down profit across Men's/Women's Apparel, Athletic Footwear, and Street Footwear.

### 2. Sales Channels & Methods
* **In-store (38%)** remains the primary revenue driver, followed by **Outlet (33%)** and **Online (29%)**.
* **Retailer Comparison:** Performance analysis across key partners including West Gear, Walmart, Sports Direct, Kohl's, Foot Locker, and Amazon.

### 3. Temporal & Spatial Trends
* **Growth Cycles:** A 2-year quarterly trend analysis showing significant revenue acceleration starting in Q1 2021.
* **Geographic Density:** A heatmap-style US map visualizing operating profit by state, identifying high-performing regions at a glance.

---

## 🛠 Features

* **Dynamic Filtering (Slicers):** * **City Slicer:** Drill down into specific urban markets (Albany, Albuquerque, etc.).
    * **State Slicer:** Filter the entire dashboard by individual US states.
* **KPI Scorecard:** Real-time visibility into Total Units Sold, Total Profit, and Active Retailer Count.
* **Interactive Visuals:** * **Pie Chart:** Sales method distribution.
    * **Bar Charts:** Side-by-side retailer and product profitability.
    * **Line Graph:** Quarter-over-quarter growth tracking.
    * **Geospatial Map:** Integrated Bing Maps visualization for regional profit distribution.

---

## 💻 Tech Stack & Tools

* **Platform:** [e.g., Microsoft Power BI / Excel / Tableau]
* **Map Integration:** Power Maps / Bing Maps API
* **Data Source:** Adidas US Sales Dataset (2020-2021)
* **Design Pattern:** High-contrast dark header with a clean grid layout for maximum readability.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1.  **Select a Region:** Use the left-hand slicers to pick a specific State or City.
2.  **Analyze Trends:** Observe the "Sales Trend By Quarters" to see how the selected region performed over time.
3.  **Optimize Strategy:** Compare "Sales Method" and "Retailers Sales" to determine the most effective distribution channel for that area.
